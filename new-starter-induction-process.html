New Starter Induction Process

To deliver an improved experience for new starters at Maidstone Borough Council.

My role in an agile delivery team

I'm the lead designer for the project and have been involved since the early discovery phase. The project team is made up of developers, a delivery manager, service designer and a product owner. I have created process maps to capture the interactions of new starters at different stages of the process.

User research

I worked with a Service Designer to interview new starters that had recently joined the Council. This allowed me to understand what their experience was like and what was lacking or proved difficult before and after they started their role

Managers across several departments were included in the user research to identify barriers that caused overly complicated processes and took up excess time.

The outcomes from these sessions were written up in a spreadsheet and transferred to a project management board in Trello.

Breaking down what happens at each stage

One of the big challenges was to look at when new starters received information or were required to complete a process.

The project team got together and carried out an extensive card sorting exercise to map all the existing processes into the following stages:

  • Pre Day 1
  • Day 1
  • Week 1
  • Month 1
  • Months 2 to 6

The outcome from the card sorting allowed me to map out new process flows to look at each stage of a new starter before presenting them to the product owner.

Discovery / Alpha

The project is in alpha and I am working on potential outcomes for improving the information that new starters receive and how they access it.
