About me

I'm Rob and I'm hugely passionate about user centered design!

My career has spanned different industries from Ministry of Justice, Met Police, Agency, Local Government and also freelance.

I've worked across design and development, as a front-end developer, digital designer, graphic designer and most currently, as a UX/UI designer.

Over the last few years my main focus has been on creating online solutions for Local Authority with users and accessibility at the center of my work.

  • Graphic designer
  • Visual design
  • Front-end development
  • Interaction design

What my focus is at the moment

When it comes to creating designs I always looked at the visual (form) instead of how it worked best for users (function). I have spent the last few years changing my paradigm when it comes to creating designs for real people.

I am a huge advocate for the Government Digital Services and as an Interaction Designer at the Ministry of Justice, I love how we create clean and transparent services for everyone - our method of working is to go out and speak to people; to find out what the real problems are when trying to do something online and to make iterative improvements to services for the public.

I'm currently undertaking a Leadership Programme as i've spent nearly 6 years as a senior designer working to develop others and help create friendly communties of design that people love to work in.
