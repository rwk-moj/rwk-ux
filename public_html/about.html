About me

I'm Rob and I passionate about all things design.

I started out designing things for the Police nearly 10 years ago and over the last few years I've spent time in the following practices:

  • Graphic Designer
  • Digital Designer
  • Web Designer / Developer
  • UX/UI Designer and Developer

What my focus is at the moment

When it comes to creating designs I always looked at the visual (form) instead of how it worked best for users (function). I have spent the last few years changing my paradigm when it comes to creating designs for real people.

I am a huge advocate for the Government Digital Services and I love how they create clean and transparent services for everyone - their method of working is to go out and speak to people; to find out what the real problems are when trying to do something online and to make continuous improvements to services for the public.

I have embodied this ethos at my current place of work and continue to drive forward the same princples of good design with a strong focus on accessibilty.
